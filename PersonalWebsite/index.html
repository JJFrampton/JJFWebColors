About Myself

I am an Object Oriented Software Development student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, currently holding a 4.0 GPA with an undergraduate degree in business from Dalhousie University. I am admittedly a bit of a nerd; I love all things in technology, computers and software.

I have been studying web technologies on my own for the past three years and just recently decided to jump into this field full time with the OOSD program at SAIT. I have been able to rapidly learn new technologies and languages and excel in their implementation because of my love for coding. I have a soft spot for working at the command line in linux and I am very confident in my abilities with SQL, C#, Java, PHP, JavaScript, and debugging. I also really enjoy working with MySQL, SQL Server, and Oracle Databases.

I am currently working on several projects outside of the SAIT program including a full stack MEAN application, an advanced website using several APIs and a simple Android game. As these projects progress they will become available for download through this site. The tools I am currently using the most often include Visual Studio, Eclipse, Android Studio, Atom, SourceTree, WAMP and LAMP.
